Vulnerabilities Prevention

Vulnerability Prevention Measures:

  • Keep your personal information secure and private.
  • Use strong, unique passwords for all your online accounts.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) whenever possible.
  • Regularly monitor your financial statements and credit reports for unusual activity.
  • Be cautious when sharing personal information online or over the phone.
  • Shred sensitive documents before disposing of them.
  • Install and update antivirus and anti-malware software on your devices.
  • Don't click on suspicious links or download attachments from unknown sources.
  • Be mindful of phishing emails and verify the sender's legitimacy.
  • Use secure, encrypted connections (https) when browsing and shopping online.
  • Secure your physical documents and cards in a safe place.
  • Be wary of public Wi-Fi networks, and use a VPN for added security.
  • Review your privacy settings on social media platforms and limit the information you share.
  • Regularly update your operating system and software to patch security vulnerabilities.
  • Dispose of electronic devices and storage media securely, ensuring all data is wiped.
